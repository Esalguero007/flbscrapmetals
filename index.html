STAINLESS STEELS

Stainless steel is very highly sought after by recyclers. Sheets, piping, tubing, turnings, and even the kitchen sink are common examples! The most common types are 304, 316, and 400 series stainless steels. The approx. make up of stainless steel type 304 is 17% chrome, 8% nickel, and 75% iron. 316 included: sheets, piping, turnings and tubing. This alloy is most commonly used in high heat and petrochemical applications. At FLB Scrap metals, we pay you top dollars to recycle
